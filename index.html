About Me

I'm David Bastida, a multimedia designer with 4 years of training at Universidad Anáhuac Oaxaca. I blend art and technology in projects involving animation, 3D design, and immersive experiences. I'm proficient in tools such as After Effects, Illustrator, Photoshop, Blender, and Maya. My focus is on visual storytelling, aesthetics, and technical excellence.
